Naomi Johndaughter Photography LLC

for proposals, engagements, weddings, and maternity!

I'm a professional couple's portrait photographer with a love for beautiful locations and natural light. I'm based in Seattle, Washington and I'm available for travel. Since buying my first camera in 2008, I've had a knack for helping others feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera. While it originally started as just a hobby, it quickly escalated! I'm detail-oriented and will make sure that you enjoy your experience AND love your photos.

In the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic, I relocated to the captivating city of Seattle, Washington. I've collaborated with the local photographers Lindsey Allison, Svetlana from SvAl Photo Art, and I have plans to assist Char Beck, and Cassie Newquist later this year. I'm currently working on finding more beautiful outdoor locations for photoshoots that are within the Seattle city limits. I'm also creating a list of tips for planning outfits and packing props for your photoshoot. Please reach out if I can help you as you pick the location and outfits!

Before moving to Seattle, I lived in Central Virginia where my photography business was featured on WAMU/NPR's Kojo Nnandi show, I assisted for wedding photographers Hunter and Sarah, photographed UVA's 2019 Fashion for a Cause fashion show, was president of Photo club at UVA (2020), and worked as a sports photographer for UVA's Intramural and Recreation photography team.

If you're interested in booking me, please contact me!
