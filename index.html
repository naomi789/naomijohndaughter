11/18/2020 update on Washington's COVID-19 guidance (read the full version):

  • Outdoor wedding receptions are capped at 30 guests (not including vendors)
  • Indoor receptions are prohibited
  • Indoor Ceremonies are limited to 30 people up to 25% capacity of the space
  • Only two people can be on the dance floor at any given time
  • Guests may mingle only if they strictly comply with the six feet physical distance and mask rules
  • Receptions cannot last longer than three hours
  • Alcohol service must end by 10pm
  • No choirs are permitted, any musicians must wear a mask
  • Make up artists must wear a face mask AND face shield

I will only photograph weddings that comply with all of these policies in order to protect myself, future clients, and other folx that I associate with.
