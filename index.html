Meet Naomi

lifestyle photographer and adventurer

I am a lifestyle photographer with a love for beautiful locations and natural light. My home is currently the small college town of Charlottesville, Virginia, but in a few months I'll be moving to the captivating city of Seattle, Washington. I love to travel and feel privileged that I have been able to travel around the world with my camera!

While living in Virginia, I have assisted for wedding photographers Hunter and Sarah, worked with the lifestyle blogger Erica Gregory, collaborated with the University of Virginia Madison House, photographed the UVA's 2019 Fashion for a Cause fashion show, was president of Photo club at UVA (2020), and worked for UVA's Intramural and Recreation photography team.

I also sell prints of my landscapes and travel photography.
