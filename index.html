Hello, I'm Naomi!

Portrait and wedding photographer

I'm a professional portrait photographer with a love for beautiful locations and natural light. I'm based in Seattle, Washington and I'm available for travel. Since buying my first camera in 2008, I've had a knack for helping others feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera. While it originally started as just a hobby, it quickly escalated! I'm detail-oriented and will make sure that you enjoy your experience AND love your photos. Please reach out if you want to book me!

Before moving to Seattle, I lived in Central Virginia where my photography business was featured on WAMU/NPR's Kojo Nnandi show, I assisted for wedding photographers Hunter and Sarah, photographed UVA's 2019 Fashion for a Cause fashion show, was president of Photo club at UVA (2020), and worked as a sports photographer for UVA's Intramural and Recreation photography team.

If you're interested in booking me, please contact me!
